In-silico apnoeic oxygen desaturation simulator

Standalone browser version of the deterministic two-compartment model used for the paediatric apnoea paper.

Educational and research simulator only. It does not predict individual patient risk and must not be used to determine safe apnoea time or guide airway/oxygenation management.
